## Competitor Watch

**Beezer Golf** — No new blog posts detected. Their blog still appears frozen (last substantive content dates to 2020-era). ZoomInfo flags them as "very low activity" compared to peers. Their App Store rating sits at 4.7 (496 ratings), but user complaints about a **6-player cap** and **aggressive subscription upsells** are notable pain points. The 6-player limit is a direct Stick differentiator — especially with Events on the roadmap. No pricing changes observed ($29.99/yr premium).

**Skins App** — No product updates or new content since the Troon partnership announcement (June 2024). The Troon relationship is still listed as active, but Troon's attention has shifted to their new "Access" platform (launched March 10, 2026) — a fully integrated tee-time booking, rewards, and e-commerce ecosystem with 200+ courses. This is a loyalty play, not a betting play, but it shows Troon investing in its own digital ecosystem rather than doubling down on SKINS. User complaints remain the same: no decimal input for handicap index (forces rounding), and no ability to run simultaneous net/gross skins games.

**18 Birdies** — No feature updates to their betting/games module. Still offering 10 games (Sixes, Skins, Wolf, Match Play, Points, Nassau, Vegas, Dots, Rolling Stroke, Targets). Their focus remains GPS-first with betting as a secondary feature. No new content published.

**GolfApp.com** — Still free, still offering Nassau, Skins, Match Play, Stroke Play, Nine Point, Closeout, Wolf, and 7 Point. Added a "Mayhem" feature (creative/custom bets like challenges and dares). No pricing changes. Minimal content presence.

**Golf Bettor** — Recent bug fix updates (back nine config loading, dismiss button, tally sheet reliability, crash fixes). Still $9.99/yr with a 60-day free trial. User reviews remain positive ("best app for managing $ golf games") but the product feels maintenance-mode, not growth-mode. 12 game formats with 65 options — impressive on paper but no content strategy to drive awareness.

**Bottom line:** Nobody has published new content. Nobody has launched new features. The window remains wide open.
