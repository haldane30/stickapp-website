## Key Reminders During Phase 1

**Consistency > Perfection:** Post on schedule even if video quality isn't perfect. Phone-quality footage is fine.

**Engagement First:** Reply to every comment and DM during first 3 months. Community beats algorithms.

**Authenticity Wins:** Share real rounds, real reactions, real mistakes. Polished content underperforms.

**Influencers are People:** Follow up genuinely. Send a free month if they post well. Build relationships, not transactions.

**Track Everything:** Note down which posts got most views, which creators drove most signups. Data informs Phase 2.

**Don't Chase Virality:** A 500-view TikTok from a micro-influencer might convert better than a 50K-view meme. Quality traffic beats vanity metrics.

**Feedback Loop:** Every week, ask yourself: "What should we do more of? Less of?"

## Tools & Templates for Execution

**Content Production:**
- CapCut (editing) + phone camera (filming) + Canva (thumbnails) = 3-tool stack
- Save time: Film 1 round per week, extract 8–10 clips, edit ahead

**Influencer Outreach:**
- Email: Use Creators.co to find creators, manual email from your Gmail
- Spreadsheet: Track outreach date, creator name, follower count, response status
- CRM: As you grow, consider Pipedrive (free tier exists)

**Measurement:**
- TikTok Analytics: Built-in
- Instagram Insights: Built-in
- Google Sheets: Track follower count, engagement rate, signups weekly
- UTM tracking: Add `?utm_source=tiktok&utm_campaign=phase1` to links

**Email:**
- Tool: Convertkit, Beehiiv, or Mailchimp (all have free tiers for <1K subscribers)
- Cadence: 1x/week minimum (Tuesday–Thursday best)
- Template: [Announcement] [Value] [Ask/CTA]
