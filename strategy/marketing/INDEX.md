## Decision Checklist (Do This First)

Before starting, decide:

- [ ] **Content creator:** Will you film content yourself or hire an editor?
  - Self: DIY with CapCut (30 min per batch, free)
  - Hire: $500–1K/month for freelancer
  - Recommendation: DIY Phase 1

- [ ] **Starting creators:** Which 3–5 creators do you already follow?
  - Pick ones you can personalize outreach for
  - These are your first targets

- [ ] **Posting schedule:** What times work for you?
  - Tues 10am–2pm, Thurs 10am–2pm, Fri 3pm (best for algorithms)
  - Lock these in before you start

- [ ] **Phase 2 budget:** If Phase 1 works and revenue flows, will you invest in Phase 2?
  - Phase 2 = $3K–5K flat-fee campaigns
  - Only proceed if you're seeing traction in Phase 1

## FAQ (Quick Answers)

**Q: Do I need a budget to start?**
A: No. Phase 1 is $0. You give away $59.99 in app access (already paid) to get influencer posts.

**Q: How long until I see results?**
A: 4–6 weeks. Consistency beats virality. Post for 6 weeks before expecting exponential growth.

**Q: What if influencers don't post?**
A: About 30% of outreach converts to "yes," and 60–70% of those actually post. So ~20% overall = 1 in 5. That's normal.

**Q: Should I pay for ads?**
A: No. Phase 1 is 100% organic. Phase 2+ can experiment with small TikTok ads ($100/week) if you want to accelerate, but organics work first.

**Q: How do I measure success?**
A: Weekly metrics (followers, posts, engagement). Monthly metrics (reach estimate, new users). By Month 3, you'll know if it's working.

**Q: What if it doesn't work?**
A: You've built 3K+ followers, 50+ evergreen content pieces, and validated what doesn't work. That's data. Use it for Phase 2 strategy.
