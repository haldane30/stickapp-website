## Key Numbers (2026 Market Data)

### Platform Performance
- **TikTok:** 7.5% engagement rate (2x Instagram), $200–$800 per post
- **Instagram Reels:** 3–6% engagement, $200–$500 per post
- **YouTube:** 4–8% engagement, $500+, but content compounds (ranks, gets shared)
- **80%** of TikTok influencer deals cost under $300

### Micro-Influencer Data
- **10K–100K followers:** Highest ROI for bootstrapped brands
- **4–10% engagement rate** (vs 1–2% for mega-influencers)
- **70% of Gen Z** relate more to micro-influencers
- **57% higher purchase rate** from micro-influencer recommendations

### Deal Structure (2026 Standard)
- **Free product:** 0% cash, 60% response rate
- **Flat fee:** $100–$500 per post, 50% higher guarantee
- **Revenue share:** 15–20% commission on sales (best long-term)
- **Hybrid:** Base fee ($200) + 10% commission (best for Phase 2)

## Why This Works for Stick

1. **Zero competition.** Every competitor sleeps on social. Stick owns it by default.
2. **Authentic fit.** Golf betting is inherently shareable. "Here's how my round went" is natural content.
3. **Micro-influencers are accessible.** They don't have agents. Email works. Free year is enticing.
4. **Low risk, high upside.** $0 cost in Phase 1. If it works (which I expect), you reinvest in Phase 2+.
5. **Compounding assets.** TikTok/YouTube content ranks over time. Links back to website. Feeds SEO. Feeds blog readership.
