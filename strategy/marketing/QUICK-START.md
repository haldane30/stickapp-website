## Red Flags (Don't Do These)

1. **Don't expect viral overnight.** Consistency beats virality. Post regularly for 6+ weeks before expecting traction.
2. **Don't spam Reddit.** One authentic comment per thread. Stop if you get banned.
3. **Don't buy followers.** Fake followers = 0 engagement = waste of money.
4. **Don't pay upfront for micro-influencers.** Free product first. Flat fee only after they've posted once organically.
5. **Don't script influencers.** They know their audience better. Give them the app, let them be authentic.
6. **Don't post low-quality content.** Phone footage is fine. Bad audio is not. Invest in a decent mic ($30).
7. **Don't chase every trend.** Consistency in your own content beats chasing trends.

---

## Green Lights (Do These)

1. **Do post consistently.** 3 posts/week, same time slot. Algorithms love predictable creators.
2. **Do engage with comments.** Reply to every comment and DM for first 3 months. Community beats algorithm.
3. **Do save clips.** Every piece of content is a building block. Save successful formats and repeat.
4. **Do track responses.** Note which influencers reply fast, which posts get highest engagement, which types convert.
5. **Do follow up once.** Template 3 after 7 days if no response. Then move on.
6. **Do build relationships.** The best outcome isn't one post — it's a creator who posts about Stick monthly because they genuinely like it.
7. **Do ask for feedback.** "What do you think?" → You'll learn what resonates.
