# Outreach Templates

> **Purpose:** Copy-paste starting points for every outreach type. Always personalize — reference something specific to them before using these.
>
> **The most important rule:** One short paragraph about them first. Then the pitch. Never lead with yourself.

---

**Subject (email):** Quick one — golf betting app you might like

I'd love to send you a TestFlight link to try it with your group. If you like it, happy to give you lifetime access and a promo code for your audience ([YOURCODE] = 1 extra free round for your followers).

No pitch, no pressure. Just think your audience would actually use it.

— Justin
[stickapp.golf](https://stickapp.golf)

---

**Notes on personalizing:**
- Mention a specific video or round they posted
- Name the specific game they were playing if you can
- Don't mention competitors
- Keep it under 150 words total — these get skimmed

## What NOT to Do

- Don't lead with "I'm a huge fan" — everyone says this
- Don't attach a PDF press kit — nobody opens them
- Don't use "synergy," "leverage," "partnership opportunity," or any corporate speak
- Don't list features before establishing why you're reaching out to them specifically
- Don't follow up more than once
- Don't pitch anyone with under 1K followers (not worth the time)
- Don't pitch anyone over 500K cold (they have management — you need a warm intro)
