## Quick Dos & Don'ts

**DO:**
- Listen to 3-4 episodes before pitching a podcast
- Personalize every pitch (mention a specific episode/article)
- Keep first pitch to 100-150 words (podcast) or 150-200 (publication)
- Lead with the problem, not the product
- Follow up once after 3-5 days if no response
- Mention Masters Week timing naturally (not as a gimmick)

**DON'T:**
- Send generic "Hey, we have a golf app!" pitches
- Pitch without doing homework on the outlet
- Use PR lingo ("disruptive," "paradigm shift," etc.)
- Make false claims (check your feature audit first)
- Pitch more than once without new information
- Expect immediate responses (2-3 week lead times are normal)
- Bad-mouth competitors (golf is a small world)
