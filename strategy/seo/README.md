## 💡 Pro Tips

1. **Batch your research.** All 8 discovery methods don't need to happen same day. Spread over week: GSC (Mon), Socrates (Fri), Reddit (daily via F5Bot), Trends (as needed).

2. **Look for clusters, not single queries.** One mention = interesting. Three mentions across different sources = content priority.

3. **Seasonal timing > content quality.** A mediocre post published 2 weeks before Masters Week beats a perfect post published 2 weeks after.

4. **Update your pipeline immediately.** When you find 3+ variants, don't wait. Add task to pipeline same day so it's on your radar.

5. **Track competitor silence.** Check if Beezer/18 Birdies publish anything. They're probably not. That's your signal to move faster.

6. **GSC is your north star.** All other research is nice-to-have. GSC tells you what's actually working. Prioritize optimizing pages already ranking.
