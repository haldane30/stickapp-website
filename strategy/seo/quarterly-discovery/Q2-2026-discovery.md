## Terms to Add to Glossary

These terms appeared in searches but are not yet in the `/guides/glossary/` page or only appear in game guides without cross-linking:

| Term | Definition | Add To |
|------|-----------|--------|
| **Air Press** | A one-hole wager called while an opponent's shot is still in the air. Must be called before the ball lands. Common in casual rounds. | Glossary, Presses guide |
| **Gimme / Gimmie** | A putt conceded by the opponent, counted as made without being putted. Standard in match play formats. | Glossary |
| **Acey Ducey** | Another name for Aces and Deuces — the low score wins from everyone, high score pays everyone | Glossary |
| **Ambrose** | Team scramble format popular in Australia and mixed-skill groups. Not a betting game per se, but often confused with Best Ball | Glossary (with disambiguation) |
| **Blitz / Sweep** | Winning all available points on a single hole. In Split Sixes or Scotch, this typically doubles or multiplies the point value. Already in game guides — needs Glossary entry | Glossary |
| **Callaway System** | An event-day handicap calculation used for corporate outings or charity events where players lack official GHIN handicaps | Glossary |

## Game Alias Updates

New alternate names and regional variations discovered through this sweep. All should be added to `game-aliases.md`.

### Nassau
- **"Aloha" confirmed:** Sometimes called as a Nassau variant on the final hole. Already in aliases.md but not fully explained. Add FAQ: "What is an Aloha bet in golf?" — common Nassau companion.

### Skins
- **"Stooges":** Rare regional slang for Skins. Low search value but worth documenting.
- **Validation Rule:** Some groups play "Validated Skins" where you must match or beat your score on the next hole to "validate" the skin. Not a different name but a named variant — "Validated Skins."

### Wolf
- **"Boss Man":** Occasionally used for the Wolf role (the person who picks partners). Low frequency.
- **"Wolfpack":** Used to describe playing Wolf with 5+ players. Very low frequency but appeared in search.

### Junk / Dots
- **"Poleys":** Appears as an alternate for "Polies" (longest putt). Spelling variation worth capturing.
- **"Greenies"** — already in aliases but confirm: "Closest in regulation" is another way people describe greenie-style bets on par 3s.

### Vegas
- **"Flip":** In some groups, when one team has a birdie, they can "flip" the two-digit score (e.g., 34 becomes 43). This is a scoring variant that produces the name "Flip Vegas" or "Vegas with Flip."
- **"Newtown":** Our aliases doc already notes Newtown — it's getting some search traffic from the "two middle balls vs outer balls" variant description.

### Scotch
- **"Umbriago":** Appeared in search results as a variant of the Umbrella game. GolfCompendium has a page on "The Umbriago Golf Game." Low frequency but worth documenting.

### Quota/Chicago
- **"Quota 36"** vs **"Chicago 39":** These search as distinct terms. Our Quota guide covers both — make sure both are in aliases.

### New game terms (not currently in any guide)
- **Bingo Bango Bongo (BBB):** Heavily searched. Should add to aliases as a game we cover educationally but don't track in Stick. Note in aliases.md with a cross-reference to any future blog post (OPP-3).
