# Weekly Discovery Log Template

Use this spreadsheet to track all keyword discoveries. Create a copy in your preferred tool (Google Sheets, Excel, Notion) and update it weekly.

## Columns to Track

| Date | Keyword | Source | Question Variants | GSC Rank | Search Intent | Stick Content? | Action | Priority | Assigned | Status |
|------|---------|--------|-------------------|----------|---------------|----------------|--------|----------|----------|--------|

## Column Definitions

**Date** — When you found this keyword (YYYY-MM-DD)

**Keyword** — The exact search term or question

**Source** — Where you found it (GSC, Answer Socrates, Reddit, Google AC, PAA, F5Bot, Trends)

**Question Variants** — If this question appears in multiple forms, list them:
- Example: "Vegas golf scoring," "how to score Vegas," "Vegas golf payout," "Vegas golf bet settlement"
- If 3+ variants → likely pillar content opportunity

**GSC Rank** — Current ranking position (if it's in GSC data)
- #1-3 = Optimize for top snippet
- #4-10 = Target with internal links
- #11-20 = Opportunity to rank better
- #21+ = Major gap, new content needed

**Search Intent** — Why someone searches this
- Learn (educational)
- Compare (vs. other games)
- Troubleshoot (settlement disputes)
- Find (best apps)
- Buy (download link)

**Stick Content?**
- ✅ YES — Link to specific page/section
- ❌ NO — Leave blank
- ~ PARTIAL — Mention in comments

**Action**
- Internal link (existing page)
- FAQ expansion (add Q&A)
- Blog post (new T1/T2 content)
- Guide expansion (expand existing game guide)
- New guide (reference guide)
- Monitor (track for 4 weeks first)

**Priority**
- 🔴 High (3+ variants, seasonal window, high search vol)
- 🟡 Medium (2 variants, no seasonal urgency, medium vol)
- 🟢 Low (1 variant, low volume, nice-to-have)

**Assigned**
- Who is writing this? (Justin, Claude, etc.)
- Leave blank if not yet assigned

**Status**
- Research (gathering info)
- Outlined (brief done)
- Drafting (writing)
- Review (Justin review)
- Published (live)
- Tracking (monitoring in GSC)

## How to Use This Template

1. **Create a master spreadsheet** (Google Sheets or Excel)
2. **Every Monday, add GSC findings** (5 min)
3. **Every Friday, add research findings** (15 min)
4. **End of month, create summary** (10 min)
5. **Quarterly, review high-priority items and assign tasks** (30 min)

---

## Tips for Effective Tracking

1. **Be specific about question variants.** If you see "Vegas golf rules," "how to score Vegas," and "Vegas golf payout" — they're all one cluster. Mark them as variants.

2. **Use GSC rank as a filter.** Rank #20 is a bigger opportunity than Rank #5. Prioritize filling gaps.

3. **Seasonal window matters.** A low-priority item becomes high-priority if it's 2 weeks before a major tournament.

4. **3+ variants = pillar content.** Write a blog post. Less than 3 = internal link or FAQ.

5. **"Monitor" is a valid status.** You don't need to act on every discovery immediately. Track it for 4 weeks, see if pattern continues. That signals demand.

6. **Update status column weekly.** If something moves from "Research" to "Drafted" to "Review" — this shows momentum.
